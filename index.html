About Us


Just like you, we were very interested in crypto investing but were overwhelmed by the sheer amount of information and lack of clear organization. As a newcomer in crypto, or even just as someone who only has time to really dive into one or two projects, it’s easy to make a bad or poorly timed investment and blow up your account. After going through this process ourselves, we began collaborating to cover more ground and using our technical and trading backgrounds to try and separate sound projects, with long-term upside, from the noise. Through this thesis driven investment strategy and a growing compilation of our learnings, UpNow Crypto Research was born.

Education



At UpNow , we understand how confusing the ever evolving crypto space can be. This is why we are dedicated to helping you understand all things crypto and ensuring you are able to make informed investment decisions. We have done this by developing UpNow University, where we offer high-level summaries of various crypto topics. In addition to these summaries, written using our own knowledge and experience in the industry, our team also has scoured the internet to find the best supplemental resources allowing you to educate yourself as much as you would like. UpNow university combines these summaries and supplemental resources to create a place that makes it easy to educate yourself on many crypto-related topics. The UpNow team is constantly working to make sure our education is up to date. Just like our reports, if you don't see something leave a comment and a team member will address it ASAP.
