Testimonials


About Us


Just like you, we were very interested in crypto investing but were overwhelmed by the sheer amount of information and lack of clear organization. As a newcomer in crypto, or even just as someone who only has time to really dive into one or two projects, it’s easy to make a bad or poorly timed investment and blow up your account. After going through this process ourselves, we began collaborating to cover more ground and using our engineering and trading backgrounds to try and separate sound projects, with long-term upside, from the noise. Through this thesis driven investment strategy, a growing compilation of our learnings, and the recognition of the potential value of a larger community, UpNow Crypto Research was born.
